FAQ

How do I make a reservation?

You can book a room directly on our website by selecting your check-in and check-out dates, choosing your room type, and completing the payment process.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of reservation. Please refer to your booking confirmation email for the specific details or contact our support team for assistance.

Is there a fee for cancellations or changes to my reservation?

To promote your real estate listings:
Cancellation or modification fees may apply depending on the terms of your booking. Some reservations are fully refundable, while others may incur fees. Please review the booking conditions before confirming your reservation.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in begins at 3:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out requests are subject to availability and may incur additional fees.

Do you offer airport shuttle services?

Yes, we offer airport shuttle services for an additional fee. Please contact us to schedule your pick-up or drop-off time in advance.

Are pets allowed in the hotel?

Pets are welcome at our hotel with prior arrangement. Please note that an additional pet cleaning fee may apply, and certain restrictions on pet size or type may exist.

Do you provide free Wi-Fi?

Yes, we offer complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the hotel for all guests.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Some of our room packages include complimentary breakfast. Please check the details of your reservation to confirm if breakfast is included.

What are the available payment methods?

We accept major credit and debit cards, bank transfers, and some digital payment options. Please contact us for specific inquiries regarding payment methods.

Can I request a special room arrangement for events or celebrations?

Yes, we can arrange special room settings for events such as anniversaries, birthdays, or honeymoons. Please inform us of your requirements when booking, and we will do our best to accommodate them.
